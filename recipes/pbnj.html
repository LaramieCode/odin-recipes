PeanutButter and Jelly

make this simple snack now!

Recipe

  • 2 peices of bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. place down your bread
  2. use a knife to spread peanut butter on 1 slice
  3. now use another clean knife to spread jelly on the other slice
  4. place slice two on top of slice one, spread facing inwards
  5. cut your crust off
  6. cut the sandwich in half
  7. enjoy!
