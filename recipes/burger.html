What is a krabby patty?

A Krabby Patty is a delicious burger that is eaten by people under the ocean, it is said to be very delicious, but no one knows the secret ingrediant.

Recipe

  • a bun
  • 3 pattys
  • seaweed
  • slice of tomato
  • slice of cheese
  • the secret ingrediant

Steps

  1. first heat up the pattys
  2. then grab out the bun
  3. open up the bun
  4. place the special ingrediant on the buns
  5. then place 2 pattys on the bottum
  6. followed by cheese than tomato
  7. now place your last patty
  8. time for the seaweed!
  9. put that top bun on and you're done
